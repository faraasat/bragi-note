# Bragi Note: Comprehensive Product Vision

## Executive Summary

Bragi Note is an AI-powered communication and comprehension assistant designed to reduce anxiety, improve clarity, and save time in three critical scenarios: catching up on missed information, communicating with emotional intelligence, and understanding complex content under stress.

**Target Market**: Knowledge workers, students, managers, remote teams, and anyone navigating high-stakes or high-stress communication.

**Core Value Proposition**: Transform information overwhelm and communication anxiety into confidence and clarity.

---

## 1. Did I Miss Anything? (Catch-Up Assistant)

### The Deep Problem

Modern work and life create constant information streams. Missing even a short period‚Äîdue to illness, vacation, focused work, or life circumstances‚Äîcreates anxiety and productivity loss. The fear isn't just about missing information; it's about:

- **Social anxiety**: Being seen as "out of the loop"
- **Professional risk**: Missing deadlines or critical decisions
- **Cognitive load**: The exhausting work of reading through hundreds of messages
- **Imposter syndrome**: Feeling like everyone else is "in the know"

### Target Users

1. **Remote Workers & Distributed Teams**

   - Multiple time zones create constant "missed conversations"
   - Async communication means missing context regularly
   - Fear of appearing disengaged when actually just in a different timezone

2. **Students**

   - Missing classes due to illness, mental health days, or scheduling conflicts
   - Difficulty catching up on lecture content and class discussions
   - Anxiety about asking "what did I miss?"

3. **Managers & Leaders**

   - Can't attend every meeting but need to stay informed
   - Need to understand team dynamics and concerns
   - Must identify action items without reading 50 Slack threads

4. **Parents & Caregivers**

   - Career interruptions for family emergencies
   - Missing work communications during childcare responsibilities
   - Need rapid catch-up during limited work windows

5. **Freelancers & Contractors**
   - Managing multiple clients means missing some conversations
   - Need to quickly context-switch between different projects
   - Can't afford to appear uninformed to any client

### Core Features

#### Input Sources

- **Meeting transcripts** (Zoom, Google Meet, Teams, Loom recordings)
- **Chat exports** (Slack, Discord, WhatsApp, Teams, Telegram)
- **Email threads** (Gmail, Outlook)
- **Shared documents** (Google Docs, Notion with comments)
- **Project management updates** (Jira, Asana, Linear comments)

#### Intelligent Analysis

The AI doesn't just summarize‚Äîit understands context and intent:

1. **Delta Analysis**: What changed vs. what you already knew

   - Identifies new information only
   - References your last interaction timestamp
   - Highlights shifts in direction or sentiment

2. **Decision Tracking**

   - Who decided what and why
   - Rationale behind decisions
   - Dissenting opinions or concerns raised
   - Implementation plans

3. **Action Items with Context**

   - What you specifically need to do
   - Why it matters (the broader context)
   - Dependencies and blockers
   - Who to coordinate with

4. **Deadline Intelligence**

   - Hard deadlines vs. soft deadlines
   - Risk assessment (what happens if missed)
   - Buffer time and planning recommendations
   - Cascading deadline impacts

5. **Sentiment & Relationship Insights**

   - Team morale indicators
   - Conflict or tension markers
   - Who needs support or acknowledgment
   - Celebration-worthy wins

6. **Questions Directed at You**
   - Direct questions you need to answer
   - Implicit questions where your input is expected
   - Urgency level of each question

#### Advanced Features

- **Relationship Graph**: Understand who's working with whom on what
- **Trend Detection**: Recurring themes or emerging issues
- **Follow-up Suggestions**: Draft responses or questions to ask

## 2. Say It Better (Communication Refinement)

### The Deep Problem

Digital communication removes social cues (tone, body language, timing) that help regulate emotional expression. This leads to:

- **Regrettable sends**: Messages sent in anger or frustration
- **Misinterpretation**: Intended meaning lost in text format
- **Career damage**: Professional relationships harmed by poor communication
- **Anxiety**: Overthinking every message, especially high-stakes ones
- **Conflict escalation**: Small issues becoming major problems

### Psychological Insight

Most people know when they're about to send a problematic message‚Äîthere's that moment of hesitation. Bragi Note intervenes in that moment, acting as an emotional regulation tool.

### Target Users

1. **Customer Support & Client-Facing Roles**

   - Dealing with angry customers while staying professional
   - Maintaining brand voice under pressure
   - De-escalating without being a doormat

2. **Managers & Team Leaders**

   - Giving critical feedback constructively
   - Setting boundaries without seeming harsh
   - Motivating without manipulation

3. **Individual Contributors**

   - Pushing back on unreasonable requests professionally
   - Advocating for themselves without seeming aggressive
   - Navigating office politics

4. **People in Personal Relationships**

   - Texting during conflicts with partners/family
   - Setting boundaries with friends
   - Addressing sensitive topics

5. **Non-Native Speakers**

   - Ensuring tone and nuance are correct
   - Avoiding cultural miscommunication
   - Building confidence in professional English

6. **Neurodivergent Users**
   - Help with interpreting and producing neurotypical communication patterns
   - Navigating unspoken social rules
   - Reducing social anxiety

### Core Features

#### Intent-Based Rewriting

User selects their goal:

- **De-escalate**: Turn down the temperature
- **Professional**: Add formal business tone
- **Warm**: Add genuine friendliness
- **Firm Boundary**: Clear "no" without aggression
- **Constructive Criticism**: Helpful feedback without harshness
- **Grateful**: Express appreciation authentically
- **Apologize**: Take accountability correctly
- **Urgent**: Convey importance without panic
- **Confident**: Remove hedging and uncertainty
- **Diplomatic**: Navigate political situations

#### Smart Preservation

The AI preserves:

- Your core message and meaning
- Important factual details
- Your authentic voice (learns your style)
- Necessary firmness (doesn't make you a pushover)

#### Tone Analysis

**Before sending**, shows you:

- How the message might be received
- Potential misinterpretation risks
- Emotional tone indicators
- Formality level assessment
- Suggestions for improvement areas

#### Version Comparison

- Side-by-side: Original vs. Refined
- Highlight changes with explanations
- Multiple alternative versions
- Explain why changes improve communication

#### Learning System

- Learns your communication style
- Understands your workplace culture
- Adapts to recipient relationship levels
- Improves suggestions over time

#### Platform Integration

- **Browser Extension**: Works in Gmail, Slack, LinkedIn, WhatsApp Web, etc.
- **Mobile Keyboard**: System-wide on iOS/Android
- **Standalone App**: Copy/paste interface

### Advanced Features

- **Relationship Context**: Different tones for boss vs. peer vs. direct report
- **Cultural Adaptation**: Adjust for different cultural communication norms
- **Historical Learning**: References past successful communications
- **Timing Suggestions**: "Consider waiting 30 minutes before sending"
- **Read Receipt Prediction**: Estimate how message will be received
- **Template Library**: Pre-built templates for common scenarios

## 3. Explain It Like I'm Stressed (Complexity Reducer)

### The Deep Problem

Complex, high-stakes information arrives when people are least equipped to process it:

- **Financial stress**: Bank notices, loan documents, tax letters
- **Legal anxiety**: Contracts, legal notices, terms of service
- **Medical fear**: Test results, insurance explanations, treatment options
- **Academic pressure**: Feedback, complex research, technical papers
- **Technical overwhelm**: Error messages, technical documentation

The complexity isn't just linguistic‚Äîit's compounded by:

- **Stress**: Cognitive function decreases under anxiety
- **Stakes**: High consequences make it harder to think clearly
- **Jargon**: Specialized language creates barriers
- **Format**: Dense, poorly structured documents

### Target Users

1. **Everyday Consumers**

   - Understanding bank and credit card notices
   - Insurance policy explanations
   - Legal documents (leases, contracts)
   - Medical information

2. **Students & Academics**

   - Complex research papers
   - Technical textbooks
   - Academic feedback and requirements
   - Scholarship and application requirements

3. **Professionals Learning New Domains**

   - Technical documentation
   - Industry regulations
   - Complex client materials
   - Interdisciplinary collaboration

4. **Non-Expert Decision Makers**

   - Board members reviewing technical proposals
   - Managers evaluating specialist reports
   - Investors reading technical analyses

5. **People in Crisis**
   - Medical diagnoses and treatment options
   - Legal situations
   - Financial emergencies

### Core Features

#### Intelligent Simplification

**Not dumbing down**‚Äîmaking accessible:

- Preserves accuracy and nuance
- Explains without patronizing
- Maintains necessary complexity where it matters

#### Multi-Layer Explanation

1. **TL;DR** (10 seconds)

   - The absolute bottom line
   - What this means for you specifically
   - Required immediate action, if any

2. **Clear Explanation** (2 minutes)

   - Plain language breakdown
   - Key concepts explained
   - Why this matters

3. **Detailed Analysis** (5+ minutes)

   - Full technical details
   - Nuances and edge cases
   - Related implications

4. **Visual Support**
   - Diagrams for complex concepts
   - Timelines for processes
   - Comparison tables

#### Specialized Modes

- **Medical**: Understanding health information with appropriate seriousness
- **Legal**: Breaking down contracts and legal language
- **Financial**: Explaining financial implications clearly
- **Technical**: Translating tech jargon
- **Academic**: Simplifying scholarly work

#### Emotional Intelligence

The AI recognizes stress signals in the text and adjusts:

- **Calming language**: Reduces anxiety through word choice
- **Empathy markers**: Acknowledges this is hard/scary/confusing
- **Action focus**: Gives clear next steps to reduce helplessness
- **Reassurance**: Points out positive aspects without toxic positivity

#### Context-Aware Analysis

- **What changed**: If you've seen similar docs before
- **Comparison**: How this differs from standard versions
- **Red flags**: Important warnings or unusual clauses
- **Opportunities**: Beneficial aspects you might miss
- **Questions to ask**: What to clarify with the sender

### Advanced Features

- **Document Annotation**: Highlights and explains complex sections inline
- **Glossary Builder**: Creates personalized term definitions
- **Follow-up Questions**: AI anticipates and answers your next questions
- **Expert Consultation Prep**: Generates questions for lawyers, doctors, etc.
- **Decision Support**: Helps evaluate options when choices are presented
- **Audio Explanation**: Listen to explanation instead of reading
- **Language Translation**: Works across multiple languages
